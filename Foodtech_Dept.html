About the Department of Food Technology and Management

Department of Food Technology and Management is established in 2020-21 at Loknete Shamrao Peje, Government College of Engineering, Ratnagiri. B.Tech in Food Technology and Management is a four year undergraduate programme having an intake capacity of 60 students. This department is affiliated to Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Technological University, Lonere, and is a unique department in the Konkan region.

What is Food Technology?

Food Technology is a field of science that deals with the techniques involved in the production of food such as production, preservation, processing, quality management, packaging, labelling and distribution of food products. The programme covers courses like food microbiology, food chemistry, beverage technology, processing of fruits and vegetables, meat, fish & poultry technology, food regulation & quality control, and food rheology & texture.

Programme Highlights

Food Technology and Management combines practical experience with theoretical knowledge to prepare students for real-world challenges, and provides opportunities to start up in the food sector. The Ministry of Food Processing Industries (MoFPI) has implemented schemes such as the Chief Minister Agriculture and Food Processing Scheme (CM AFPS), Pradhan Mantri Kisan SAMPADA Yojana (PMKSY), PM Formalisation of Micro food processing Enterprises (PMFME) Scheme, and the Production Linked Incentive Scheme for Food Processing Industry (PLISFPI), offering financial, technical, and business aid to establish food processing enterprises.

Research in the food technology field leads to improved food products that are both safe and nutritious, and helps students think critically about project ideas, gather and analyse data, and draw sound conclusions. During summer and winter vacations, students undergo a one month internship, and in the final semester a six month internship, linking academic learning with real-world application in food production, safety, and innovation.

The department maintains strong industry-institution interaction with national and international food companies such as Gadre Marine (Ratnagiri), AMUL (Anand), Britannia (Pune), Nexton Foods (Pune), Nestle (Switzerland), Desai Foods (Pune), Hindustan Foods (Mumbai), and ITC Ltd. (Ranjangaon).

Career Opportunities

Graduates of the B.Tech in Food Technology and Management will be well-prepared for careers in various sectors, including:

  • Food Processing Industries: production, preservation and value addition of food products.
  • Quality Assurance & Quality Control: ensuring safety, hygiene, and regulatory compliance.
  • Research & Development: and regulatory agencies working on food innovation and standards.
  • Supply Chain Management: across sourcing, processing, packaging, and distribution.
  • Academia and Research Institutions: teaching and research in food science.
  • Food Corporation of India and other public sector food agencies.
  • Food Safety & Standards Authority of India (FSSAI) and related regulatory bodies.

Welcome to the Department of Food Technology and Management!

Department of Food Technology at GCOER was established in academic year 2020-21, offering a four year undergraduate (UG) degree programme (B.Tech) in Food Technology. It is a unique department in the Konkan region, aiming to equip students with the knowledge and skills to address challenges in the food industry, from research and development to quality control and food safety.

The B.Tech programme in Food Technology sits at the intersection of science, innovation, and business, playing a critical role in ensuring food security, safety, quality, and sustainability for a growing global population. Food Technology is a dynamic field, offering diverse and exciting career paths, from developing novel food products and optimizing production processes to managing supply chains and ensuring regulatory compliance. Students also explore various food processing technologies, including their benefits and limitations, to understand their impact on food quality and safety.

Food technology courses lead to a variety of job profiles including Food Scientist, Food Technologist, Quality Assurance Manager, and Food Safety Officer. The department has well experienced faculty members who are experts in various areas of food science, engineering, and management, and they are here to support students' learning journey.

The institute interacts with various food industries like Gadre Marine, Britannia, Nestle, Amul, and Nexton for internships, training, and placements for students. This education provides opportunities to do a start-up in the food sector.

Direction

Vision & Mission

Vision

"Empowering food technologists to enhance global food security, improve health, and produce value added products."

Mission

  1. To educate and develop skilled food technologists in a dynamic, technology driven environment.
  2. To produce competent food technologists with sound knowledge of hygienic food processing, preservation, food standards and regulations, packaging and storage in order to reduce food losses.
  3. To impart knowledge through research and education and to groom them as professionals to meet the ever increasing demand of society.
  4. To develop future professionals equipped to innovate and ensure safe, sustainable, and nutritious food for global communities.
