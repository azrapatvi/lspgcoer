About the Institute

A young institute, a serious mission.

Lokenete Shamrao Peje Government College of Engineering, Ratnagiri was established in 2021 as an institute of the Government of Maharashtra, affiliated to Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Technological University, Lonere, District Raigad. The college is built to develop technologically competent human resources who go on to serve society, with a strong emphasis on academic rigour, research, and industry-linked training.
